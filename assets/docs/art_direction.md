# Art Direction

## Project

`The Adventures of Foxman, a Merciless Bastard`

## Visual Target

Adult satirical dark cartoon fantasy for a fast 2D side-scrolling action game. The world should feel grimy, theatrical, corrupt, and ridiculous, but gameplay readability is the boss.

## Visual Pillars

### Filthy Storybook Violence

The world looks hand-painted, stained, chipped, and badly maintained. It has fairy-tale shapes, but the fairy tale is run by corrupt civic clerks, drunk guards, bile pipes, and suspicious taverns.

### Foxman Is Charming Trash

Foxman should read instantly as fox/man hybrid:

- Fox ears.
- Sharp snout.
- Expressive tail.
- Human posture.
- Ragged coat or scavenged fantasy gear.
- Predatory lean.
- Mean grin.

He should look agile, rude, and just respectable enough to make his behavior worse.

### Satirical Low-Fantasy Decay

Primary world target: `Rotten Borough: Municipal Gutworks`.

Signals:

- Tavern alleys.
- Sewer arches.
- Civic tax notices.
- Bribe boxes.
- Badly patched stone.
- Rusted pipes.
- Rotten banners.
- Defaced heroic murals.
- Fake prophecy merchandise.

### Readable Mayhem

Combat clarity wins over joke density.

Rules:

- Foreground actors must have clear silhouettes.
- Backgrounds should be desaturated and lower contrast than characters.
- VFX should bloom briefly, then get out of the way.
- Hazards should have a distinct color/value language.
- UI should be dirty but legible.

## Content Boundaries

Allowed:

- Profanity-coded attitude.
- Gross fantasy grime.
- Comedic violence.
- Corrupt bureaucracy satire.
- Mean jokes about fictional villains, institutions, and Foxman's own behavior.

Avoid:

- Explicit sexual imagery.
- Protected-class slurs.
- Hate symbols.
- Real-person likenesses.
- Real-world political symbols.
- AI-generated fake text unless it is intentionally reviewed and cleaned.

## Rendering Style

- Hand-painted cartoon game art.
- Exaggerated shapes.
- Strong silhouettes.
- Painted grime and material texture.
- Not photoreal.
- Not strict retro pixel art.
- Runtime sprites should support transparent backgrounds and atlas packing.

## First Biome

Name: `Rotten Borough: Municipal Gutworks`

Mood:

- Dirty amber tavern light.
- Sewer greens.
- Bruised purple shadows.
- Tarnished brass.
- Muddy stone.
- Red hit accents.

Gameplay lane:

- Characters and hazards must read against the environment.
- Background detail should sit behind the play plane.
- Foreground collision should use bold edges.

## First Character Targets

### Foxman

Ragged fox/man antihero with a springy silhouette, sharp snout, expressive ears, and tail. Should look nimble enough to dodge and cruel enough to insult a corpse, without becoming visually monstrous.

### Drunken Guard

Corrupt municipal guard. Rounder silhouette than Foxman, helmet or civic badge, rusty weapon, sloppy posture, readable swing windup.

### Sewer Rat Brute

Compact, hunched, gross, fast shape. Should be readable as a small enemy at 96x96.
